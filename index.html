ABOUT THE MARKET

The Wellington Night Market is a weekly celebration of cuisine, culture, and creativity on Cuba Street. A dining destination and a MUST DO experience for city locals and visitors to the world's 'coolest little capital'. It's all about food, flavours, fun, friends, and family.
